About


Hello everyone, I am Wu Xidong. Currently in my sophomore year, I am studying in a joint training program between Northeastern University School of Medicine and Dundee University in the UK. My major is biomedical engineering. Biomedical engineering is an interdisciplinary field that integrates knowledge from multiple fields such as medicine, biology, and engineering, committed to solving practical problems in the field of healthcare. My research focuses mainly on the analysis of medical signals and medical images. And use artificial intelligence algorithms for computer-aided diagnosis and treatment decision-making. The projects I have participated in include the construction and deployment of classification models for electrocardiogram signals, as well as the application of SAM large models in medical images.
